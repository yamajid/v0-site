VEED vs Crayo: Different Stages, Both Essential

This isn't an either/or comparison. VEED builds your homepage demo. Crayo builds your TikTok pipeline. You need both — just at different points in the funnel.

April 9, 2026 8 min read Tested on Fedora 43

The Short Answer

Use VEED for your homepage. It produces polished, 16:9 product walkthroughs that go in your hero section or pricing page. Use Crayo for your social media traffic. It generates 9:16 short-form clips with auto-captions designed for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts. These aren't competitors — they're different stages of the same funnel.

▶ Use VEED When…

You need a professional product demo for your landing page hero section, pricing page, or investor pitch. VEED gives you timeline editing, voiceover, auto-captions, and export at 4K.

Use cases: homepage hero video, product walkthrough, onboarding tutorial, investor demo, YouTube channel content.

Best for: conversion-critical videos. This lives on your website and directly influences purchasing decisions.

▶ Use Crayo When…

You need high-volume social content to drive top-of-funnel traffic. Crayo takes a single screen recording and produces 10+ TikTok-ready clips with auto-captions, split screens, and viral hooks in 2 minutes.

Use cases: TikTok product teasers, Reels "build in public" clips, YouTube Shorts, dev community content, launch announcements.

Best for: discovery. This is your customer acquisition engine — volume over polish, reach over production value.
The Pipeline Playbook

Record your v0-built site once with OBS. Upload to VEED for your hero video (16:9, polished, with voiceover). Upload the same recording to Crayo for 10 TikTok clips (9:16, auto-captions, split screen). One recording, two outputs, full funnel coverage.
