v0 Pricing Guide 2026: Complete Plan Comparison

Updated April 6, 2026 10 min read

Vercel v0 offers five pricing tiers designed for different use cases, from hobbyists exploring AI code generation to enterprises requiring advanced security. This guide breaks down exactly what you get at each level.

How Credits Work

Credits function like a prepaid balance in v0:

  • Each plan includes monthly credits that reset with your billing cycle
  • Generations draw from your balance based on model and token usage
  • Unused credits roll over to the next billing cycle (expire after 65 days)
  • Premium, Team, and Business can purchase additional credits anytime

Plan Details

Free Plan - $0/month

Perfect for exploring v0's capabilities. Includes $5 in monthly credits, 200 projects limit, and basic usage analytics. No team features or shared projects.

Premium Plan - $20/month

For power users who need more. Includes $20 in monthly credits, unlimited projects, and the ability to purchase additional credits. Still individual-only (no team features).

Team Plan - $30/user/month

For collaborative teams. Includes $30/user in credits, shared credit pool, shared projects, basic access controls, centralized billing, and team-wide usage analytics.

Business Plan - $100/user/month

For privacy-conscious organizations. All Team features plus data opt-out - your content won't be used for model training.

Enterprise Plan - Custom pricing

For large companies. Includes SAML SSO, RBAC (role-based access control), advanced usage analytics, priority access, and custom credit allocations.

Technical Limits

  • Context window: 128,000 tokens max input
  • Image inputs: ~1,000 tokens each
  • Output size: 32,000 tokens max per response

Which Plan Should You Choose?

Choose Free if you're learning v0 or building small personal projects.

Choose Premium if you're a solo developer or freelancer who needs more capacity.

Choose Team if you work with others and need shared projects and credit pools.

Choose Business if you handle sensitive data and need training data opt-out.

Choose Enterprise if you need SSO, RBAC, and dedicated support.
