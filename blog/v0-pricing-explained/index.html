v0 Pricing Explained: Every Plan, Hidden Costs & Verdict (2026)

Updated April 7, 2026 10 min read

Table of Contents

Plans at a Glance How Credits Work Hidden Costs Tips to Save Which Plan

v0 uses a credit-based pricing model. Here's the full breakdown as of April 2026.

Plans at a Glance

Free Plan — $0/month

  • $5 in monthly credits (about 10-15 generations)
  • Access to v0-1.5-md model
  • GitHub sync available
  • Vercel deployment included
  • Best for: experimentation and learning

Premium Plan — $20/month

  • $20 in credits monthly (4x the free tier)
  • Access to v0-1.5-lg model (higher quality)
  • Figma import feature
  • Higher attachment limits
  • Full API access
  • Best for: serious developers and frequent users

Team Plan — $30/user/month

  • $30 credits per user monthly
  • Shared workspace with real-time collaboration
  • Team chat features
  • Centralized billing
  • API access for all members
  • Best for: teams building together
Credit Math

A simple UI component costs $0.10-$0.30. A full landing page costs $0.50-$1.50. A complex multi-page app can cost $2-$5 per generation.

How v0 Credits Actually Work

Credits are consumed per generation. When you type a prompt and v0 generates code, it deducts credits based on the complexity and output size. The key insight: better prompts = fewer credits spent. A well-crafted single prompt that gets it right the first time is vastly cheaper than 5 iterations of guessing.

Are There Hidden Costs?

  • Over-usage beyond included credits costs additional money on Premium and Team plans
  • Each retry and re-generation uses more credits
  • Large attachments (images, Figma files) consume more credits than text-only prompts
  • Complex multi-step generations cost significantly more than simple component prompts
  • Credits don't roll over between months

Tips to Save on Credits

  • Write detailed prompts upfront — use the product surface, context, constraints framework
  • Use Design Mode for visual tweaks instead of re-generating
  • Start with the Free plan to learn the prompting workflow before committing
  • Plan your generations before building to minimize retries
  • Save your best prompts as templates for reuse across projects

Which Plan Should You Choose?

Stick with Free if: you're learning v0, using it occasionally for side projects, or primarily testing and prototyping.

Upgrade to Premium if: you use v0 more than 2-3 times per week, need the higher-quality output, or rely on Figma import.

Go Team if: you have multiple people collaborating on v0 projects daily and need shared workspaces and centralized billing.

v0 Pricing vs Competitors

v0 Premium at $20/mo is competitive with Bolt.new ($20/mo) and Lovable ($25/mo). The credit system is more flexible but requires more planning than fixed-token models.

Frequently Asked Questions

v0 offers a free plan with $5 in monthly credits, Premium at $20/month, and Team at $30 per user per month.

Premium and Team users can purchase additional credits. Free users wait for the monthly reset.

As of April 2026, v0 does not offer an annual billing discount.
