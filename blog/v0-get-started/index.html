Get Started With v0: Your First 10 Minutes

Updated April 7, 2026 6 min read

Table of Contents

Go from zero to your first deployed v0 component in exactly 10 minutes. No setup required.

Minutes 1-2: Sign Up and First Prompt

Go to v0.app. Sign in with Vercel or GitHub. You'll land on a blank canvas with $5 in free credits.

Type: "Create a modern landing page hero with a bold headline, subheadline, two CTA buttons, and trust badges. Dark theme."

Minutes 3-4: See the Preview

The right panel shows your generated UI. It should look professional immediately — that's shadcn/ui at work.

Minutes 5-6: Iterate

Refine: "Make the headline gradient. Add an animated background." Or use Design Mode for visual tweaks — zero credits.

Minutes 7-8: Second Component and Export

Generate pricing cards. Click Export or GitHub sync to download your code.

Minutes 9-10: Deploy

Click "Deploy to Vercel." Live URL in 60 seconds. Share it with your team.
